Scenario #1

Art Contest

Scenario:

Both you and your friend Thomas are the finalists for an art competition with a prize of $1,000 for the winner!

You know Thomas has not worked nearly as hard as you on his art, but you also know that he is way too busy to be able to invest as much time as you because he works several jobs to provide a source of income for his family.

You and Thomas stay up late the night before the competition to put the finishing touches on your paintings. Thomas stays after you as he still needs more time.

The next morning, you come to the art studio to pick up your painting for the competition.

However, you become extremely upset when you find that your beautiful painting is placed exactly where you left it, but now it is covered with black streaks, covering your intricate floral design and ruining all of your hard work!

What should you do?
