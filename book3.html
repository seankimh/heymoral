Scenario#3

Drugs

Scenario:

After school you see your friend, James, surrounded by a few other boys standing in a circle at the back of the school.

Interested to see what is happening you walk a little bit closer and stand on your tippy toes to get a better look of the situation.

You see that James with a look of discomfort. His eyes widened with horror.

You see someone forcefully handing James a drug, telling him to consume it.

Unable to escape the circle and with no trusted adults near by, James takes the drug hesitantly.

Through peer pressure, the bully had made your friend take a drug when had no intentions to do so.

What should you do?
