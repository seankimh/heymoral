Scenario #2

Social Media

Scenario:

It's a Friday afternoon and you come home to sit at the comfort of your couch. You decide to rest by spending some time on the Instagram social media app.

While browsing Instagram on your phone, you come across an Instagram account that you heard some kids chattering about at school earlier that day. Wanting to see what the account was about, you click on the account to view the profile.

Surprisingly, the account was not you anticipated. The account has many posts making fun of your peers, calling them rude names.

You look to see the comments on one of the posts making fun of your friend, Lizzy.

In the comments you see that Lizzy commented, "This hurts my feelings. Can you take down this post?" The post, however, remains undeleted.

What should you do?
